## What is Convex?

```text
Convex is a reward-boosting layer built around Curve-related liquidity strategies. In practice, users deposit supported LP tokens into Convex to access boosted CRV rewards, CVX rewards, and sometimes extra incentives without having to personally manage veCRV.
```

## Convex vs Curve

```text
Curve direct gives you more raw control, but Convex is usually the simpler route if you want boosted rewards without managing the boost mechanics yourself.
```
